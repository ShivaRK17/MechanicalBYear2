FLUID MECHANICS AND HYDRAULIC MACHINES LABORATORY

List of Experiments

  • Calibration ofVenturimeter, Orifice meter (discharge measuring device in pipes)
  • Calibration of Orifice and mouthpiece ( discharge measuring device in Tanks)
  • Calibration of triangular notch and rectangular notch (discharge measuring device in Channels)
  • Measurement of viscosity by Hazen-Poiseuille method and Stokes method
  • Determination of Darcy's friction factor, relative roughness for laminar and turbulent flows
  • Application of momentum equation for determination of coefficient of impact of jets on flat and curved blades and Pelton bucket
  • Determination of Manning's and Chezy's coefficients for smooth and rough channels
  • Determination of energy loss in hydraulic jump
  • Determination Velocity distributions in open channels Computation of pressure drag coefficient for flow past a cylinder in a subonic wind tunnel
  • Performance Characteristics of single stage centrifugal Pump, multi stage centrifugal pump, Submersible pumps, and varying speed centrifugal pump
  • Performance Characteristics of Pelton turbine, and Francis turbine
